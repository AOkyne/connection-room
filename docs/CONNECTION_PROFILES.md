# Connection Profiles

## Overview

Connection Profiles are self-awareness archetypes that help members understand their personal approach to vulnerability, intimacy, and authentic connection. These profiles are **not about dating compatibility** but rather about recognizing one's own relational patterns and how one shows up in deep connection with others.

## Current Connection Profile Archetypes

These 12 archetypes describe different approaches to connection and vulnerability:

1. **The Nurturer** - Shows up as a caregiver; finds meaning in supporting others' growth and healing
2. **The Explorer** - Curious and open; willing to venture into new territories of connection and desire
3. **The Philosopher** - Seeks meaning and deeper understanding; values intellectual and spiritual exploration
4. **The Seeker** - On a journey of self-discovery; exploring identity and authenticity
5. **The Conscious** - Brings intentionality and awareness; values explicit communication and consent
6. **The Harmonizer** - Focuses on balance and equilibrium in relationships; seeks stability and flow
7. **The Bridge Builder** - Connects different perspectives; comfortable with complexity and nuance
8. **The Romantic** - Seeks authentic love and deep emotional connection; idealistic about relationships
9. **The Awakening** - Going through transformative personal growth; learning and evolving
10. **The Mystic** - Integrates spirituality with intimacy; sees connection as sacred
11. **The Healer** - Facilitates emotional and relational healing; creates safe spaces for vulnerability
12. **The Transformer** - Navigates personal transformation; supports others through change

## Future Implementation

### Quiz Logic (Not Yet Implemented)

The Connection Profile should be determined by a self-discovery quiz with questions about:
- How you approach vulnerability in groups
- What role you naturally take in intimate conversations
- How you support others' growth and transformation
- Your relationship to your own personal evolution
- What "deep connection" means to you
- How you experience intimacy (emotional, physical, spiritual)
- Your communication style in vulnerable moments

### Profile Descriptions (Not Yet Implemented)

Each profile needs:
- **Core approach**: How this person relates to connection
- **Strengths in community**: What they bring to The Connection Room
- **Growth edge**: Areas for continued development
- **Affinity**: Which other profiles they naturally align with
- **How to show up**: Practical guidance for this archetype

### Scope Notes

- This is a **self-awareness tool**, not a matching system
- Profiles should emphasize vulnerability, authenticity, and personal growth
- Language should be inclusive of all genders, orientations, and relationship structures
- The quiz should be optional but encouraged during onboarding
- Profiles should be re-assessable (members can retake the quiz)
